Normalizing styles have been helped along thanks to the fine work of Nicolas Gallagher and Jonathan Neal https://necolas.github.io/normalize.css/

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
1. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
1. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
